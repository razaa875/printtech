Company Profile

Printech Packages, PTP, is a complete printing and packaging solution company. We believe in providing high quality packaging products to aid and enhance the efficiency and profitability of the product of our clientele.

With a plant covering an area of over 100,000 sq ft, PTP is a hub of modern technology and one of the most efficient work forces in the market.

We at Printech believe in providing a one window solution to our clients. Hence our machine line extends from Rotogravure, Flexo, Offset, Extrusion and Corrugation, allowing PTP to provide solution to our customers who range from Food & Beverages, Cosmetics, Textile, Retail and Bulk consumers. The customized corporate, printing & packaging solutions help our business partners all over the country in streamlining the flow of goods, product fortification and innovative and extraordinary packaging presentation.

PTP's focus and endeavor has today made us one of the most preferred vendors of FMCG and MNC companies all over Pakistan.

Rotogravure

We are commercially driven and focus on excellence with impact.

With three flexible printing less, PTP has the capacity to provide our customers with quick turnaround time. Our facility is open 24/7 for our customers to bring their vision on any and every kind of flexible substrate and form it into a product of their desire. Our rotogravure department provides full services in printed and laminated rolls, film, 3 & 4 layer al-foil rolls, retort pouches , surface printed PE, shrinkable PET & PVC sleeves, etc.

Offset

We are commercially driven and focus on excellence with impact.

PTP offset department also operate within our philosophy of quick service and timely deliveries to our clients and we are only able to do so with our 10 printing machines, in-house designing and plate making facility. Our offset product line extends from cardboard boxes to POS material and corporate brochures.

We also offer corrugation packaging solution for heavy and bulk packaging.

Extrusion

We are commercially driven and focus on excellence with impact.

PTP is a market leader in providing customized solution in LDPE for our customers. We understand that it is not a one size fit all product strategy. It is all about the chemistry and we know it. With five extrusion lines, PTP makes sure that multiple customers are addressed at the same time and the product is made to customer specifications.

Quality Control

We are commercially driven and focus on excellence with impact.

Quality Control is our utmost priority. Keeping in line with our mission, PTP has three individual QC departments keeping quality checks on our Rotogravure, Offset and Extrusion departments.

Our Clients

Company

Established in 2010 in Karachi, Pakistan, PTP is in the packaging industry to render services to our customers with proficient and unmatched quality backed by our efficient after sales services.

Printech Packages has seen exponential growth in a short span of 5 years, starting with a single line in 2010, PTP has inducted 2 more printing lines. Parallel to Rotogravure expansion, PTP has developed a complete range of services for it's customers, which include, Offset unit, Extrusion, Corrugation departments with the support of in-house designing department to ensure accurate results befitting individual job-requirement.

We are focused on providing our customers with a one stop solution for their Flexible and Paper & Board based printing and packaging needs.

Built on the strong foundations of a clear vision, team work, and innovation, we stress on the true model of total customer satisfaction.
